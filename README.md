<h2>About The Project</h2>

<h3>Tied to a sad life</h3>
You play as a salaryman depressed by his monotonous life.
To cheer himself up, he decides to go to a party, and for that he needs the best tie he can find.
<h3>A mysterious encounter</h3>
He sets off in search of a store when he meets the gaze of a ghost wearing a superb tie, who promptly leaves for the abandoned mansion at the end of the street.
Your goal is to get into the mansion and find the best tie of all time, but it's not going to be a typical mansion...
